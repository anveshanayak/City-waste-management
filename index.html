SOCH

The primary goal of SOCH is Promoting community-wide cleanliness in order to maintain a clean, safe environment for the society to reside in.

We wish to assist every city in becoming a smart city with a sophisticated waste management system and have hygienic surroundings.

HOW are we going to do it:

  • 100% door to door collection and storage of waste accros the city

  • To put our very own modern smart bins on streets to tackle the problem of street waste managements

  • Establishment of modern garbage transfer stations at strategic locations

  • Food Exchange,community cleaning drives to safegueard our envirenment

  • live garbage-Truck tracker

FAQ's

What if i missed the door-to-door garbage collection?

You can always go drop to one of our 'Waste collection station' which is local to every community

What is the Frequency of the door-to-door pick up?

we pick up garbage from door-to-door every morning, You can always track our garbage trucks from our tracker

what can i buy from reward points?

you can buy jute bags from our products page

How can u contribute as an individual?

You can sign up for our community garbage drives or donate on our website

Any further related queries can be shared with us in our contact page
